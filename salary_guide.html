    SDE:

    • Software development engineers (SDE) work in a range of industries to apply the principles and techniques of computer engineering, information analysis, and computer science to design, build, and develop their employers' computer systems and software.
    • An SDE I is essentially a new and inexperienced (at the level of Amazon's engineers) junior engineer, usually a college hire.
    • An SDE II s have a few years of experience, including experience with large codebases and some architectural experience.
    • An SDE IIIs have many years of experience, and have led or spearheaded at least one nontrivial or important project.

      65+ = Principal SDE = Makes complex problems disappear.
